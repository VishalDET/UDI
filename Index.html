About Us

United Distributors Incorporation (UDI) is a leading name in the distribution of premium international brands across India. With a legacy of excellence and a commitment to quality, we bring the world’s finest products to your doorstep. Our extensive portfolio spans categories like food, beverages, personal care, and more, ensuring we cater to diverse consumer preferences and needs.

As a trusted partner to global brands, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service, excellence in distribution, building strong relationships, and fostering brand growth in the Indian market. At UDI, our mission is to enrich lives with products that inspire trust and joy. read more
