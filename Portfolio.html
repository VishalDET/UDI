Our Brand Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Nationwide Reach.

Our journey began with a single brand, driven by the vision of creating high-quality products that meet the needs of our customers. Over time, we expanded our product offerings and brand presence, building a foundation that would support growth and innovation in every step of the way.

From the launch of our first brand, we have continuously evolved. We invested in cutting-edge technology, improved our distribution channels, and leveraged strategic partnerships to expand our portfolio. Today, we manage multiple brands across the country, each contributing to our legacy of excellence.

Our expansion has been powered by state-of-the-art infrastructure, which includes automated warehousing, temperature-controlled storage, and AI-powered inventory management. With these advanced systems in place, we ensure seamless distribution, allowing each brand to thrive in its respective market.

Our partnership with United Distributors has transformed our market reach and operational efficiency.

Sarah Johnson, CEO of Barilla

Brands Partners

We carefully select our brand partners to ensure we deliver only the highest quality products to our customers.

Loacker

Italian confectionery company

Loacker nowadays has two plants, one in Auna di Sotto on the Renon and a second one in Heinfels in East Tyrol (Austria). At both production sites wafers and chocolate specialties are produced at more than 1,000 meters above sea level. Today, the third generation leads the company: Armin's sons Andreas and Martin Loacker together with his nephew Ulrich Zuenelli who works as executive chairman.
