Brand History

Since 1925, the Loacker family has been crafting delicious wafer and chocolate specialties in the heart of the Italian Alps. What began as a small bakery in Bolzano has grown into a global brand known for quality and taste.

For generations, we've maintained our commitment to natural ingredients, authentic recipes, and sustainable practices - creating pure moments of enjoyment for millions of people worldwide.

Why Loacker

Our Italian hazelnut orchards

We only use 100% Italian hazelnuts, which partially grow in our own orchards.

Our Cocoa Farming Program

With our project we support cooperatives and farmers in Ecuador and the Ivory Coast.

Our NON GMO Alpine Milk

Our milk is 100% from small mountain farms in the Alps and is NON GMO.

Our Bourbon vanilla from Madagascar

Working with local small farmers makes our vanilla sustainable, transparent and fair.
