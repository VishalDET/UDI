Brand History

Founded in 1980, the Zino Davidoff Group is a Swiss-based family business. Our founder, Zino Davidoff, is at the heart of the brand and inspires everything we do - our product collections reflect his natural elegance and appreciation for fine quality, style, authenticity and good living.

Zino valued simplicity and wanted to share the happiness that comes from enjoying beautiful things - and it was through his products that he set out to share this spirit with the world.

Today, the name Davidoff is synonymous with pleasure, timeless style, good living, modesty and joie de vivre. Davidoff Cafe guarantees creating unique moments of taste and pleasure with every cup of coffee.
